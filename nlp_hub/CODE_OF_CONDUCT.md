# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people who want to contribute to NLP Hub, regardless of their identity, background, or experience level.

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, socioeconomic status, and technical ability.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Being patient and understanding with newcomers
- Providing helpful and supportive feedback

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination based on any protected characteristic
- Violence or threats of violence
- Intimidation or bullying

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at siddoyacinetech227@gmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Reporting Guidelines

When reporting a violation:
1. **Email**: siddoyacinetech227@gmail.com with subject `[CODE OF CONDUCT VIOLATION]`
2. **Include**: 
   - Description of the incident
   - Names of involved parties
   - Relevant links or evidence
   - Your contact information
3. **Keep confidential**: Please maintain confidentiality until the matter is resolved

### Investigation Process

- Initial response: Within 24-48 hours
- Investigation period: Up to 7 days
- Decision communication: Within 14 days
- Action: May include warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban

## Enforcement Actions

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension of contributions
- Permanent ban from the community
- Legal action if laws are violated

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all NLP Hub community spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- GitHub Discussions
- Discord/Slack (if applicable)
- Community events
- Email communications

## Inclusive Language

We are committed to using inclusive language. Some examples:
- Use "they/them" as a gender-neutral singular pronoun
- Avoid gendered terms like "guys" or "ladies"
- Be mindful of cultural differences
- Use accessible language
